About

I'm a full-stack developer who lives at the intersection of mobile and web. Over the last five years I've built and shipped products across marketplaces, healthcare, HR, real estate, e-commerce and more.

I specialize in React Native for iOS & Android and React.js / Next.js on the web, and I'm comfortable owning the backend in Node, NestJS and Express when the team needs it. I'm drawn to scalable monorepos, reusable SDK-style packages, and the unglamorous work that makes a codebase pleasant to grow.

As a team lead, I gather requirements straight from clients, break the work down, and keep shipping alongside the team — then handle the release to the App Store and Play Store myself.
